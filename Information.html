EXO

A South Korean-Chinese boy group that was formed by Sm Entertainment in 2011 and debuted on 2012. Exo was originally consisted of 12 members namely Suho, Xiumin, Kris, Tao, Lay, Luhan, Baekhyun, Chen, Chanyeol, D.O, Kai and lastly their maknae, Sehun.

Exo was also devided into two groups. Exo-K, which produces Korean songs and Exo-M that produces Mandarin version of their songs.

What's the name of their fandom?

During the years 2012 and 2013, fans used to call themselves Exotic or just Exo stans. But gladly, 2 years after their debut, the official fandom name was finally revealed. Exo called their fans Exo-L.


The 'L' in Exo-L stands for Love. But the letter 'L' actually means a lot. First of all, 'L' was the 12th letter that connects K (Exo-K) and M (Exo-M). Exo-L was actually considered as a sub unit of Exo. In all, there are mainly 3 sub-unities for Exo. Exo-K for one that promotes in Korea, Exo-M in China, and Exo-L for promoting worldwide love for the two. That's also where the band's slogan "WE ARE ONE" came from, saying that Exo-K, L and M were ONE whole Exo.

For some reasons, after 2 years of their debut, 3 Chinese members left the group. It was kind of a sad story, but the other EXO members continued to strive. In 2018, their 6th album 'Don't Mess Up My Tempo', became their highest charting Album on Us Billboard 200, debuting at 23, and their best selling album in South Korea, on where they sold over 1.9 million copies.
